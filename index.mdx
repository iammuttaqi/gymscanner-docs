# Create Your Profile on Gymscanner
#### [Made by Gymscanner Inc. with Scribe](https://scribehow.com/shared/Create_Your_Profile_on_Gymscanner__WOCQgoPdQjilg51YCcE4bg)
This guide provides a straightforward process for creating your vendor profile on Gymscanner, ensuring you present yourself effectively to potential clients. By following the outlined steps, you can enhance your visibility and credibility on the platform. Additionally, a well-crafted profile can attract more users and foster better connections within the fitness community.

1\. Navigate to [https://gymscanner.com/v/profile/info](https://gymscanner.com/v/profile/info)

![](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-06-02/7e189424-f50e-4d26-bd1a-45de467898b7/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=82,70&br_px=1458,840&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0)


2\. Click "Profile"

![](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-06-02/7e189424-f50e-4d26-bd1a-45de467898b7/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=0,0&br_px=1376,769&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=16,66)


3\. Upload / Edit Your Profile Image

![](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-06-02/9cd412ac-79dd-4cbf-abcb-52981288d283/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=0,0&br_px=1376,769&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=349,213)

4\. Complete all sections of your profile

![](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-06-02/de1dc663-1f83-4e7e-b0e8-ee6e6f0cfa89/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=164,0&br_px=1541,769&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=1079,38)
